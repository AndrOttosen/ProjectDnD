Plan your campaign!

Here you can Plan your Campaign which consist of setting up the campaign, adding characters to the campaign, adding your scenes and lastly ofcourse inviting your friends to join you on your adventure!

If you click Setup Campaign you'll be able to start customizing which order the scenes will be played and which characters are gonna be in the campaign and their roles.

Characters in Campaign will allow you to add which characters should be in the campaign

Your Scenes is where you can add which scenes you want the campaign to contain.

Lastly Invite Friends is where you'll be able to invite the friends you want to join you in the campaign!
