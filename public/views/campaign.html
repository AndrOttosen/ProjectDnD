Campaign!

Welcome! On this page you'll be able to plan your campaigns, look at your currently ongoing campaigns, look at the scenes you've written and lastly if you dont want to go through the hassle of making your own campaign, you can always try our preset campaigns!

If you click Plan Campaign you'll be able to start customizing your own campaign.

In Current Campaign you'll be able to view your currently ongoing campaigns.

Your Scenes is where you can write and store your scenes for the campaigns.

Preset Campaigns cotains a set of different already made and ready to play!
